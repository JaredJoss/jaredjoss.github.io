## Detailed Research & Publications

### Primary Research: LLM Safety & Healthcare

**1. Insights from the Inverse: Reconstructing LLM Training Goals Through Inverse Reinforcement Learning**
- **Venue:** COLM 2025
- **Tech Stack:** TRL, Hugging Face, Pythia (70M/410M), RLHF.
- **Key Findings:** Developed a max-margin Inverse Reinforcement Learning (IRL) algorithm to extract reward models from fine-tuned LLMs.
  - The algorithm achieved **80.40% accuracy** in recovering the ground-truth objective.
  - Demonstrated that fine-tuning with the extracted IRL reward model resulted in **lower toxicity** than the original RLHF process.
- **Link:** https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&citation_for_view=1Eh35coAAAAJ:9yKSN-GCB0IC
